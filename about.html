About Me

Hello, I’m Vishwa Chandra Chakma – a passionate technologist from Diyun, Arunachal Pradesh with a deep-rooted interest in innovation and technology. I have developed strong skills in programming and web development through hands-on projects and real-world experiences.

I am currently pursuing my B.Tech at Shreeyash College of Engineering and Technology. During my internship at CDSP's in Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar, I gained valuable experience working with Arduino Uno, the 8051 microcontroller, and Python.

I am proficient in C, C++, Python, and SQL, and I enjoy creating responsive web applications using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My projects—including a real-time RFID Attendance System and an Todo lists web pages using html,css and js my ability to blend hardware and software to solve real-world problems.

Beyond technology, I am an avid runner, traveler, and meditation enthusiast, which helps me maintain balance and fuel my passion for continuous learning.
